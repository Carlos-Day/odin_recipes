Crispy Tofu Bowl

Deliciously crispy and savory tofu.


This is a quick and easy recipe for baking tofu in an oven. Press the water out of the tofu then cut it into small squares. Mix all of the other ingredients in a bowl and then toss the tofu in the bowl. Then bake for 16-20 minutes at 400 degrees.

Ingredients

  • 15oz block of tofu
  • 1 tb soy sauce (or tamari)
  • 1 tb olive oil
  • 1 tb corn starch (or starch of choice)
  • Optional:
    • Red pepper flakes
    • Nutritional Yeast
    • Smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Press the tofu. Try to get as much water out as you can. Then cut it into small cubes.
  2. Mix the soy sauce, olive oil, and corn starch in a bowl.
  3. Toss the tofu in the oil and starch mixture so all of the cubes are evenly covered. Add other spices like red pepper flakes to taste. (no nutritional yeast yet)
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
